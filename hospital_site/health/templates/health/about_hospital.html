About Us

Cessa's Health Foundation is a non-profit healthcare organization serving the diverse the diverse community and beyond. With pioneering medical research achievements, education programs defining the future of healthcare, and wide-ranging community benefit activities, we are setting new standards for quality and innovation in patient care.

Growing with C.H.Fd.

It began in 2016 as a 12-bed clinic in Seibel Street, New Road, Borikiri. Today, Cessa's Health Foundation serves more than 100,000 people each year in over 20 locations, with more than 450 physicians and nurses and 100 research projects in motion. What never changes is our commitment to be a blessing for those in need.
